# Missions

Missions introduce the core mechanics of the Crypto Hunters game.  They are
divided into four tiers of difficulty: Easy, Normal, Expert and Genius.

## Easy Missions

* **E1 – Hello Hash**
  - Objective: Compute simple SHA‑256 hashes.
  - Reward: 100 DRX.

* **E2 – Nonce Nudge**
  - Objective: Find a nonce that yields a hash below a small target.
  - Reward: 100 DRX.

* **E3 – Block Anatomy**
  - Objective: Assemble block header fields in their correct order.
  - Reward: 100 DRX.

## Normal Missions

* **N1 – Merkle Scout**
  - Objective: Rebuild a Merkle root from supplied leaf hashes.
  - Reward: 250 DRX.

* **N2 – Auth Check**
  - Objective: Implement HMAC verification for a message.
  - Reward: 250 DRX.

* **N3 – Replay Guard**
  - Objective: Detect and block replay attempts in an API context.
  - Reward: 250 DRX.

## Expert Missions

* **X1 – TX Validator**
  - Objective: Simulate transaction validation rules of a decentralised ledger.
  - Reward: 500 DRX.

* **X2 – Protocol Patch**
  - Objective: Patch a vulnerable routine in a consensus protocol.
  - Reward: 500 DRX.

* **X3 – Rate‑Limit Forge**
  - Objective: Tune anti‑bot rate limits to prevent denial of service.
  - Reward: 500 DRX.

## Genius Missions

* **G1 – Anomaly Hunt**
  - Objective: Detect threats hidden in noisy telemetry streams.
  - Reward: 1,000 DRX.

* **G2 – Consensus Siege**
  - Objective: Defend the consensus mechanism while under coordinated attack.
  - Reward: 1,000 DRX.

* **G3 – Zero‑Day Drill**
  - Objective: Respond to a zero‑day exploit affecting multiple shards.
  - Reward: 1,000 DRX.
